## 🆕 D28 实施核心要点

### 1. StatisticsDao 5 方法(2 小时)

**新文件**:`lib/data/db/daos/statistics_dao.dart`(~190 行 + codegen 后 + part directive)

5 方法:
- `getMonthlyIncome(DateTime)` — 过滤 type=income + `excludeFromIncomeExpense=false` + occurredAt in [start, end)
- `getMonthlyExpense(DateTime)` — 同理过滤 expense
- `getMonthlyBalance(DateTime)` — income - expense(本月净额)
- `getCategoryBudgetUsed(int categoryId, DateTime)` — 过滤 expense + `excludeFromBudget=false`(S04 预算前置)
- `getMonthlyStats(DateTime)` — 聚合 snapshot(income/expense/count/excluded)— 主页卡片用
